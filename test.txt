
i1. Start the Application

  cd /Users/erolakarsu/projects/AIPricingOptimizer
  ./start.sh

  Then open http://localhost:3000

  2. Login

  - Click "Use Demo Credentials" button (fills email/password)
  - Click "Sign In"

  3. Testing Each Feature

  AI Competitor Price Tracker (/competitor-tracking)

  1. Click "Competitor Tracker" in sidebar (or card on Dashboard)
  2. View data: See table with 16 seeded records
  3. Row click: Click any row → Detail modal opens
  4. AI Analysis: In modal, click "Generate AI Analysis" button
  5. Edit: Click "Edit" button in modal
  6. Delete: Click "Delete" button in modal
  7. New item: Click "+ New Tracking" button at top

  AI Demand Forecaster (/demand-forecasts)

  1. Click "Demand Forecaster" in sidebar
  2. Generate AI Forecast:
    - Select a product from dropdown
    - Click "Generate Forecast" button
  3. View: Click any row to see details + AI analysis
  4. Edit/Delete: Buttons in detail modal
  5. New item: Click "+ New Forecast" button

  AI Bundle Recommender (/bundle-recommendations)

  1. Click "Bundle Recommender" in sidebar
  2. Generate AI Bundles: Click "AI Generate Bundles" button (green)
  3. View: Click any row to see bundle details
  4. Activate: Click "Activate Bundle" button in modal
  5. Edit/Delete: Buttons in detail modal
  6. New item: Click "+ New Bundle" button

  AI Discount Optimizer (/discount-optimizations)

  1. Click "Discount Optimizer" in sidebar
  2. Generate AI Optimization:
    - Select a product from dropdown
    - Click "Calculate Optimal Discount" button
  3. View: Click any row to see discount analysis
  4. Edit/Delete: Buttons in detail modal
  5. New item: Click "+ New Optimization" button

  AI Price Elasticity Analyzer (/price-elasticity)

  1. Click "Price Elasticity" in sidebar
  2. Analyze Elasticity:
    - Select a product from dropdown
    - Click "Analyze Elasticity" button
  3. View: Click any row to see elasticity details + AI recommendations
  4. Edit/Delete: Buttons in detail modal
  5. New item: Click "+ New Analysis" button

  Quick Test Flow

  1. Login → Dashboard
  2. Click "AI Demand Forecaster" card
  3. Select "Wireless Bluetooth Headphones" from dropdown
  4. Click "Generate Forecast"
  5. Wait for AI response (shows structured analysis)
  6. Click row to see full details
  7. Test Edit/Delete buttons

  The AI buttons will call OpenRouter API and display results in a professional formatted view (not raw JSON).
